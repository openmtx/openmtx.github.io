Welcome to the open matrix.

For a long time this domain sat empty. The name was a joke — *Open Matrix*, because I'd been calling myself agent smith in chat windows and it stuck. Now it's becoming something else: a place to write about AI in the open.

## Why the brackets

The wordmark is `[[ openmtx ]]`. The double brackets are the notation for a matrix in math, and the indexing operator in Python and Rust. They're a small claim: this is a place for people who think in vectors, weights, and shapes.

It's also, of course, a nod to a movie about AI. I'm not going to pretend otherwise. But the serious version of the joke is that we now *live* inside systems that are part math, part myth — and writing about them clearly is its own kind of work.

## What to expect

- Research notes as I go, not just polished essays
- Code, where it helps
- Arguments I haven't finished
- Occasional footnotes to a 1999 film
